Fried rice Recipe

Home

Description

Nigerian Fried Rice is a colourful, flavorful rice dish stir-fried with mixed vegetable, spices, and often served with grilled or fried chicken. it's made by first cooking rice in a seasoned stock, then tossing it with stir-fired veggies, liver or shrimp, and curry-infused oil.

Ingredients

Main Ingredients

  • Long-grain parboiled rice
  • Chicken stock
  • Chicken
  • Vegetable oil or butter
  • Liver or shrimp

Vegetables

  • Carrots
  • Green beans
  • Sweet corn
  • Green peas
  • Spring onions
  • Green bell peppers

Seasoning & Spices

  • Thyme
  • Curry powder
  • Salt
  • Garlic and ginger
  • Seasoning cubes

Steps

  1. Prepare the stock by boiling the chicken with onions, seasoning cubes, curry powder, thyme and salt.Once cooked, separate the chicken and reserve the stcok.
  2. Add curry powder to the chicken stcok and add the rice to cook until it is about 90% done. Drain excess liquid if necessary and set aside.
  3. Stir-fry the vegetables.
  4. Combine the rice and Vegetables.
  5. Stir in diced liver or shrimp and mix well.
  6. Serve hot with grilled or fried chicken, salad, or plantains.
