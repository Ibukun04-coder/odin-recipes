Egusi Soup Recipe

Home

Description

Egusi Soup is a hearty and flavouful West African dish made from ground melon seeds cooked in a rich blend of palm oil, vegetables, and spices. Popular in Nigeria, it is often paired with sawllows like pounded yam, fufu, or eba.

Ingredients

Main Ingredients

  • Ground egusi
  • Palm oil
  • Meat
  • Stockfish and dried fish
  • Crayfish
  • Leafy vegetables
  • Onions
  • Fresh pepper
  • Meat stock or water

Seasoning & Spices

  • Salt
  • Seasoning cubes
  • Ground pepper
  • Locust beans

Steps

  1. Prepare the proteins with onions, salt, seasoning cubes, and pepper. Reserve stock.
  2. Prepare the egusi paste by mixing it with a little water.
  3. Fry the egusi in a hot palm oil, add chopped onions. Fry on medium haet, stirring continously until it begins to dry and form crumbs.
  4. Add meat stock to the fried egusi and stirr well. Simmer for 5-10 minutes.
  5. Add vegetables.
  6. Adjust seasoning if needed. Serve hot with pounded yam, eba, fufu, or semovita.
